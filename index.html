About me

I’m a postdoctoral researcher at the Swedish Institute for Social Research (SOFI) at Stockholm University. I recently defended my PhD thesis, Essays on the Determinants and Measurement of Subjective Well-Being.

I’m broadly interested in the economics and psychology of subjective well-being (SWB). My research concerns SWB in relation to economic conditions and time use, and different issues related to the measurement and conceptualization of SWB.
